Noise modelling

Experimental project on modeling noise environmental map for the center of Moscow. Produced using fantastic tool NoiseModelling leaded by acousticians from the Joint Research Unit in Environmental Acoustics (UMRAE, Université Gustave Eiffel - Cerema) and Geographic Information Science specialists from Lab-STICC laboratory (CNRS - DECIDE Team).

It’s model is built on CNOSSOS-EU document. And we used a lot of OpenStreetMap data (mostly road network and buildings)
