About

I am a Geospatial data analyst and engineer with more than 7 years of experience, mostly in urban planning and risk management spheres. I have gained additional expertise through graduating as a Civil Engineer and working as an Urban Planner for several years.
I specialise in helping to organise and analyse data for making insights and the right decisions. I have experience working in remote teams using Agile methodology. I am flexible and detail-oriented. I love contributing to OpenStreetMap and looking for new challenging opportunities.

My stack for spatial analysis and visualisation

  • ESRI ecosystem (ArcMap, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Server, AGOL, etc)
  • Python (Pandas, Geopandas, ArcPy, PyQgis, etc)
  • PostGIS/PostgeSQL
  • FME Server
  • GDAL
  • Qgis

I also have strong experience with

  • Geoserver
  • Autodesk products (including Map3D and Infraworks)
  • Mapbox stack
  • MapInfo
